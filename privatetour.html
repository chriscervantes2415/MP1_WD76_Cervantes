Sorsogon Top Tourist Spots Private Tour with Lunch & Transfers from Legazpi City


Avail now for 3400 PHP only

Description


Explore Sorsogon and witness the province's most iconic attractions on this full-day private tour from Legazpi. Visit San Benon Hot Springs, Old Barcelona Church, Kambal Busay Falls, Balay Buhay Sa Uma Bee Farm, Sorsogon Museum, and Bulusan Nature Park with the comfort of the package's included private air-conditioned van transport. You will also enjoy a delicious complimentary lunch.


Your tour will begin once you are picked up at your accommodation in Legazpi City. You will be driven to the town of Irosin. Here you will find the San Benon Hot Springs, which sits at the foot of Bulusan Volcano. You can go for a dip into the naturally heated water and relieve your stress and muscle pains.


After a relaxing pool bath, you will be paying a visit to one of the region's oldest churches, Old Barcelona Church. Constructed in 1874 by the Franciscan friars, the church walls were initially made of coral rocks. The interiors underwent several renovations throughout the years. Today, you will notice that the outside of the church is purely made of stones while the inside has steel and glass features.


Next in your itinerary is the jewel of this tour, the municipality of Bulusan. Bulusan is one of the top eco-tourism destinations in the Philippines. It is filled with natural wonders like mountains, forests, rivers, falls, lakes, springs, and white-sand beaches.


Bulusan will reward you with its vibrant ecosystem and stunning natural attractions, starting with the highest peak in the province, Mount Bulusan. The slopes of this volcano shelter local industries like Karagumoy handicrafts, honey farms, and Pili plantations. You will also drop by the Bulusan Volcano National Park, Palogtok Falls, and Bulusan Lake, among others.


Also in Bulusan is Kambal Busay Falls and Balay Buhay Bee Sa Uma Farm. The former is a gorgeous waterfall that features several picturesque cascades, while the latter is an agri-tourism site that is home to accommodations, a restaurant, and a bee farm. You will also visit Sorsogon City so you can explore the Sorsogon Museum, which used to be the provincial jail of Sorsogon but is now home to exhibits that showcase the history and culture of the province.


If your group is into surfing and would like to take lessons or try out some basics of this sport, it's recommended to visit Gubat in the morning before heading to the attractions mentioned above.


Come along on this exciting tour and experience Sorsogon's top tourist spots. Check the availability by choosing a date.
