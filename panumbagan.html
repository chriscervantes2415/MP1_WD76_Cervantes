Description


Live the island life for a day and go on an off-the-beaten-path water adventure at Panumbagan Sandbar in Sorsogon through this day tour. For travelers fond of the sea, sun, and sand, this exciting day tour is something you should not miss. This tour also comes with the service of a guide, boat transfers, complimentary lunch with refreshments, rental snorkeling gear, and roundtrip land transfers within Sorsogon.


From your accommodation, you will head to the town of Pilar for registration and a quick briefing. Once you’re all settled, you can then board the boat waiting for you at the Pilar Port and set sail for the Panumbagan Sandbar. This sandbar features a five-kilometer sandy passageway on the main shoreline. Its pebbly white sand is dotted with tiny pockets of greenery, adding to its beauty. Additionally, its shallow waters allow you to spot sea urchins and sea grapes you can eat as fresh snacks.


Once you arrive at the sandbar, you will be greeted by its clear waters and powdery shores. Feel free to wade in the waters or lounge on the beach. Don’t forget to try snorkeling, too! Pilar has a nearby reef teeming with aquatic life that you should definitely check out. Your guide will be more than happy to point you to the best sights. They will also bring you to see a small cave to see more of the area.


Apart from the beautiful sandbar, the place has much more to offer. If you’re fortunate enough to have a clear view during your trip, its strategic location will show you a breathtaking sight of two of the most famous volcanoes in Bicol, the Mayon and Bulusan Volcanoes. You can also stay out at Panumbagan until late in the afternoon and watch the sunset while dozens of egrets pass by the beach. Afterward, you will make your way back to the mainland of Pilar.


Unwind on the Panumbagan Sandbar in Sorsogon by booking this full-day tour. Check the availability by choosing a date.
