Avail now for 4000 PHP only

Description


Get your beach essentials and cameras ready for a memorable visit to the postcard-worthy sights of Matnog, Sorsogon. If basking under the sun and chilling by the beach sounds like a great time to you, a day tour to Matnog is a nice stopover for your trip to Bicol. This tour lasts for about 10 hours and includes the service of a guide, boat transfers, a complimentary lunch, and pick-up and drop-off within Legazpi or Dataga City.


The municipality of Matnog is situated in the southernmost part of Luzon in the Bicol region. Here, you'll find pristine and unspoiled beaches with powdery sand and crystal-clear water. This day tour includes visits to Tikling Island, Juag Marine Sanctuary, and other must-see island spots.


On the day of your tour, your guide will fetch you from your accommodation within Legazpi or Daraga City at 6 AM. After settling in your transfer, you'll travel to your first stop: the famous Tikling Island, which boasts a pink sand beach. It's less crowded than the more popular beaches in the region, which means you have more privacy here. Feel free to stroll along the shore, swim in shallow waters, take pictures, or lounge and soak up some sun.


From there, you'll head to Juag Marine Sanctuary for a slice of marine life during your trip. It is a must-visit for families as everyone can learn about marine animals during their time here. Aside from admiring the various fish that are cared for here, you can also buy a cup of fish food to feed them. After checking out the aquatic life here, you can ask your boatman to bring you to the other pink sand beaches in the area.


Discover the best beaches in Matnog during your Bicol vacation by booking this day tour. Check the availability by choosing a date.
